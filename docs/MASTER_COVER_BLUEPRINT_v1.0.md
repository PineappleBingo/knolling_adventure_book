## 1. CANVAS CONFIGURATION
The cover is generated as a **Single Seamless Image**.
* **Total Width:** 17.365 inches (43.6mm)
* **Total Height:** 8.75 inches (22.2mm)
* **Spine Zone:** Center 0.115 inches. Keep critical text away from the spine folds.
* **Safe Zone:** Keep all critical text/logos 0.375" away from the outer Trim Edge.
